EISI PRODUCTS.

Our products are known throughout the library and education industry as leading brands, with a reputation of quality. We further enhance this with local support, installation and training to enable Philippine librarians and educators to apply these technologies to their needs immediately.

Library Automation


We are the leading supplier of Library Automation solutions in the Philippines with Follett Software's Destiny Library Manager (DLM). Our web-based DLM is used by major libraries in the Philippines and is supported by our professional staff. We also provide cataloging tools, transaction trackers, barcode supplies and peripherals.
